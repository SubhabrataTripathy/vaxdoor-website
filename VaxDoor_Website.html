How VaxDoor Works

From booking to vaccination — the entire process takes under 3 minutes. Here's how it goes.

Simple Process

4 easy steps to get vaccinated

1🔍
Browse & Select
Browse our full catalogue. Filter by age group, disease, or brand. Read what each vaccine is for.
2📍
Enter Location
Enter your home address, pincode, or drop a pin. We'll show you available time slots near you.
3💳
Pay Securely
Pay via UPI, card, or net banking. 100% secure Razorpay gateway. You'll get an instant confirmation.
4💉
Get Vaccinated
The nurse arrives, verifies ID, administers the vaccine. Digital certificate is auto-generated immediately.
More Details

Everything handled for you

🌡️
Cold Chain Guaranteed
All vaccines are stored at WHO-mandated 2–8°C. Every vaccinator carries a temperature-logged cold pack. We never compromise on vaccine integrity.
📍
Live Nurse Tracking
Once assigned, you can track your vaccinator in real-time on a map — exactly like tracking a Swiggy delivery. Know exactly when they'll arrive.
🧾
Instant Certificate
Your digital vaccination certificate is generated the moment the nurse marks the dose as administered. Download it as PDF or share directly to WhatsApp.
🆘
Adverse Reaction Protocol
Our nurses are trained for post-vaccination monitoring. If any adverse reaction occurs, our SOS protocol connects you to an emergency doctor within minutes.
